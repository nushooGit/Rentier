# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally phased to protect the MVP from overbuilding.

## Phase 1 MVP Landlord

Goal: give landlords a usable foundation for managing rental properties.

- Organization/workspace setup.
- Landlord dashboard.
- Property CRUD.
- Lease CRUD and lease status tracking.
- Renter association through users and invitations.
- Manual rent payment records.
- Payment status tracking.
- Property expense records.
- Basic maintenance ticket tracking.
- Basic document metadata and upload flow when storage is ready.
- Mobile-first landlord workflows.
- Policy-backed authorization for all landlord resources.

Success means a landlord can track properties, leases, payments, expenses, and maintenance without spreadsheets.

## Phase 2 Renter Portal

Goal: give renters a focused portal connected to existing landlord-managed data.

- Renter dashboard.
- View active and past leases.
- View rent payment status and history.
- Submit and track maintenance tickets.
- View shared documents.
- Message landlord or organization contacts.
- Receive notifications for important updates.
- Policy-backed renter access to only relevant records.

Success means a renter can understand their lease, payment status, documents, and maintenance requests without contacting the landlord for basic information.

## Phase 3 Utilities Automation

Goal: support manual utility workflows and prepare for later automation.

- Utility providers.
- Utility accounts per property or lease.
- Utility bills with billing periods, due dates, amounts, status, and attachments.
- Utility readings for meters where applicable.
- Reminders for due bills and missing readings.
- Renter visibility for relevant utility obligations.
- Basic utility reporting by property and period.

Success means landlords can track utility bills and readings reliably even before official integrations exist.

## Phase 4 Official Provider Integrations

Goal: reduce manual work through supported integrations.

- Payment processor integration for rent collection.
- Official utility provider integrations where available.
- Email and SMS notification providers.
- Object storage provider for documents.
- Accounting export or integration.
- E-signature integration for leases and documents.
- Integration health monitoring and retry strategy.

Success means integrations save time without weakening data ownership, security, or reliability.

## Phase 5 Advanced SaaS Features

Goal: mature Rentier into a broader SaaS product after core workflows are proven.

- Subscription billing and plan limits.
- Advanced roles and permissions.
- Multi-organization workflows for property managers.
- Audit logs and activity history.
- Advanced reporting and exports.
- Bulk import and migration tools.
- Custom notification preferences.
- More complete document lifecycle.
- Admin and support tooling.
- Production observability and operational dashboards.

Success means Rentier can support larger landlords, property managers, and production operations without compromising the core product.
